Why are more people working from home?
And what problems can arise in the long term?

Percentage of employees working from home in Germany (Source: eurostat).

  • Flexibility

    Ability to choose work hours and pace, leading to better time management.

  • Social Isolation

    Less face-to-face interaction can lead to loneliness or lack of belonging.

  • No Commute

    Saves time and money, reduces stress from traffic or public transport.

  • Blurring of Work-Life Boundaries

    Home becomes office, making it hard to separate work and personal life.

  • Environmental Benefits

    Less vehicle usage decreases environmental impact.

  • Communication Challenges

    Remote communication may cause misunderstanding or delays in information sharing. Household distractions (TV, chores, family) can lower concentration

Meeting Rooms

WiHaMen has not only quiet and uninterrupted rooms for video conference but also has rooms with all the equipment for Virtual Reality (VR) meetings.

Virtual Reality (VR) Meeting

WiHaMen offers rooms with all the equipment for our VR meeting software. WiHaMenVR is very easy to use because of its simplicity. VR controllers are not needed. You use only your hands and a keyboard that you are already familier with. WiHaMenVR is designed for small team meetings (up to 8 people). The meetings take place in a cabin with beautiful mountain views. If some members want to have a more private discussion afterward, they can go downstairs to a cozy, comfortable space.

You can feel their presence around you and communicate with them through eye contact.

Video Meeting

If you attend a big conference, so you don't need a close conntact with people, you can use the quiet and uninterrupted rooms for your meeting. There is a webcam if you don't have a proper one. There won't be anyone who interrupt you while your meeting and you also won't bother your family or roomates.

Co-working offices

Big screens are available. You can just bring your laptop and work here. You can decide whether you work with a mobile place if your teammates are your neighbors or in a silent space if you prefer more focus.

freestyle office

All the desks and chairs are mobile. If your teammates are in the same WiHaMen branch or you are collaborating with other companies, This office is here for you. You can move your desk and find a spot where you want to work with others. All of the seats have a big monitor, so you can work more efficiently.

silent office

Some people just need a quiet place to focus on their work. This office has even bigger monitor with a privacy panel.

neighbor community

Since you all live close to a WiHaMen branch, you are basically neighbors. You can take yoga or meditation courses together or are free to build your own clubs (vollyball, board game, ets.). WiHaMen offers a place for your physical and mental well-being.

Dining Rooms and Courses

There are indoor and outdoor dining places for your lunch or a cup of coffee. WiHaMen provides yoga and mediation courses to support your physical and mental well-being. You can also take communication courses. You don't have to be scared of finding someone to eat together.

Clubs

The daily grind would be slowly wearing you down if you don't have any other activities except from your work. To find small everyday accomplishments, you can connect with people who don’t necessarily work with you but share the same interests. If you already have a hobby, it feels great to help others learn about it. If you don’t have one yet, you can discover a new hobby here with your neighbors. WiHaMenStory offers a funtion that allows people to upload temporary posts to find neighbors for their clubs. You can either upload a post or send an email to find others who enjoy the same hobbies!

Branches

You can chose where to live with an affordable apartment. The workplace is close enough to commute with a bike. You can decide when to start and finish without considering your boss and colleages.

Close office

As a remote worker, it can be difficult to completely leave the city and country where your company is located. However, living in the center of a big city is expensive, and the dense population can be stressful. WiHaMen branches are located in the place where you can save on rent, stay connected to the city, and enjoy a natural environment. Let’s commute to work by bike!
